Our Solutions

YouthShield is a service designed to monitor and filter web searches, sifting through content that nobody else can to provide the best risk assessment and preventative measures

Smart Monitoring

We filter content through 345 granular categories to ensure every piece of content is being monitored and labeled

Different Types of Content

YouthShield scans across all types of content, ranging from web searches to videos, and from chatbots to audio, ensuring we cover every piece of content students are consuming

Risk Scores

YouthShield calculates a risk score for every student based on content consumed and external factors, leading to an accurate and predictive model to enact preventative protection

Incident Reporting

YouthShield has the ability to report events that are done in person, such as bullying, and other events that can contribute to a student's safety and risk score

Chatbots

YouthShield has the unique aspect that allows for us to monitor AI chatbots that students are talking to, allowing us to get a better understanding of what students are looking for and go deeper into what types of content they view

Early Detection

YouthShield provides early-warning detection for risky behaviors, sending alerts and showing high risk students and what kinds of risk they pose to themselves or to others in order to alert staff and have intervention before it becomes a problem

Offline Updates

YouthShield combines data from online with offline information like grades for more holistic student profiles, allowing us to understand what a student is beyond just what they search up online, and understand what really we need to do in order to keep them safe and secure
