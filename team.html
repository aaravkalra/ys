Our Team

Aarav Kalra

10th Grade, Saratoga High School

Aarav is a high school sophomore with a unique mix of technical skills, leadership, and community service. As captain of his school’s Lincoln-Douglas Debate team, he’s grown the program nearly fivefold. A dedicated member of the marching band, he combines discipline and creativity in his performances and leadership. On the FTC robotics team, he develops coding solutions for complex challenges. Beyond technology, Aarav volunteers teaching math and English to children in low-income communities, empowering the next generation through education.

Maxime Sobrier

10th Grade, Saratoga High School

Maxime is a high school sophomore with a strong interest in artificial intelligence and software engineering, a passion he has developed through active participation in FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) Robotics since seventh grade. A native speaker of French, Mandarin, and English, Maxime serves as the treasurer of his high school’s FLUENT Translation Club and has volunteered over 200 hours in translation and online English tutoring. Additionally, he is an Eagle Scout, reflecting his commitment to leadership and community service.
